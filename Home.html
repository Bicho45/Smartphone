The Smartphone

A smartphone is known as a mobile phone device that allows the user to do some things other than basic ones that include dial-up, and
smartphones work with touch screens, and there are many applications and games that can be run on this type of phone,and the first phone
has been launched Smart was introduced to the market in the year 1949 1994 by IBM

and it contains calculator applications(1),calendar(2),address book(3) and many other applications, and
smartphone has many advantages and benefits that benefit the user, and among these advantages are the following:

  1. Capture and store photos and videos on the device.
  2. Browse the Internet and access the email service.
  3. The ability to use the Global Positioning System (GPS) through a smartphone.
  4. Listen to sound and music.
  5. Use alarm clock and clock.
  6. View weather and temperature information.
